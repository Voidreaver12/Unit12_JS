Unit12_JS - The Three Challenges

In this activity, you must complete all 3 of the challenges below to earn your prize.

Challenge #1 - Same Game

In this challenge, you will play a simple matching game. Click on a group of clustered tiles with the same color to break them. Larger clusters earn more points. You must complete this challenge with a score of 1000 to pass this challenge!

Challenge #2 - Snake

In this challenge, you will play the classic Snake game. Eat food to grow longer, and avoid running into walls or eating yourself. You must reach a snake length of 50 to pass this challenge!

Challenge #3 - Puzzle

In this challenge, you will complete a simple jigsaw puzzle. You must fully complete the puzzle to pass this challenge!
